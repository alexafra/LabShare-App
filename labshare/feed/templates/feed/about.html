LabShare

The objective of this application is to minimise the wastage of resources after they are used in Medical Science based labs at the University of Western Australia. More specifically, this will be accomplished via an iOS based mobile gift-economy marketplace. Different members and departments of the UWA medical faculty can share resources falling into the three major categories of reagents, expertise, and equipment. The application will streamline the process of finding resources, such that university members can efficiently search for products they are looking for, accomplished with an intuitive user interface and fully functional front and backend features. Additionally, it should save the university money and reduce environmental wastage, by reducing situations in which duplicate resources are acquired.
